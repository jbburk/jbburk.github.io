Welcome to Lake Geneva

Each and every member that joins the Lake Geneva Chamber of Commerce is an investment in a growing community and in your business. Together, we will work to make Lake Geneva the best community in all southeastern Wisconsin to grow communities and careers.

The Lake Geneva Chamber of Commerce sponsors the most popular events of the year. From the world-famous ice-sculpting competition in the Winter to Venetian festival in the Summer. Our Chamber of Commerce even takes part in the Walworth County Fair, which, is well known to be the best county fair in the state.

You can rest assured that we at the Lake Geneva Chamber of Commerce have your business needs in mind. Take a stroll through downtown Lake Geneva and you'll see for yourself that Lake Geneva offers events, colors, tastes, and wonders for each and every season. While, you're there why don't you stop by our office and see exactly how your business can grow with the Lake Geneva Chamber of Commerce.
