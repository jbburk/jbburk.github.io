Site Purpose

The name for the site will be Rainy Day Weather. The domain name for the site is rainydayweather.com which costs $12.95 a year.

The purpose of this website is to educate its users about potentially hazardous weather in an areas forcast. This serves the community by informing them about potential problematic weather so they can feel safe and prepared to go about their business. To inform our users we will include up-to-date weather forcasts and weather news articles.
